About Us

Welcome to FoodSocket, your number one source for all things groceries. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of food products, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2025 by John Adeloye, FoodSocket has come a long way from its beginnings in a small home office. When John first started out, his passion for providing fresh and affordable groceries drove him to do intense research and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Ibadan and its environs, and we are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the grocery industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
